# Registering account and Logging-in

FastTrack 2 shares account authentication information with Backend.AI. A Backend.AI account is **required** to use FastTrack 2 MLOps. If an account does not exist, a Backend.AI account must be created before accessing the service.

## Registering account

* FastTrack 2 provides a single button for both initial registration and login.
* When accessing the site for the first time, click the **Sign in with Backend.AI** to register.
* Once a Backend.AI account is linked to FastTrack 2, same button can be used for further logins.

<figure><img src="../../images/image (22).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

① **Sign in with Backend.AI**: If Backend.AI account is already registered, it can be used as a credentials.

② **Sign up now**: If no account exists, click **Sign up now** to create a new Backend.AI account.

## Connecting the Backend.AI account to FastTrack 2

<figure><img src="../../images/image (23).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Account connection confirmation popup</p></figcaption></figure>

When linking a Backend.AI account to FastTrack 2 for the first time, an additional account connection popup may appear. If this happens, please follow the steps provided.

* Click the orange **Login** button at the bottom right to proceed with authentication.&#x20;
* To sign in with a different account, click the white **Sign in with a different account** button at the bottom left.
